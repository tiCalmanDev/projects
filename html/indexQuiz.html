Coffee Quiz

Rules(functionality)

  • There are three questions in this quiz using a mutliple choice, radio input with a single answer
  • There is a hint function for each question, with a clue specific for each question
  • There is a forward and back button
  • You may review your answers and change them at the end
  • You can see all hints when on the review page
  • Press the submit button to to submit your answers
  • You can retake the quiz by pressing the restart button
  • There is a progress bar at the top to track which question you are on
  • There is a countdown timer that will begin once you press start quiz and submit whichever questions you have answered when the 30 seconds is up
  • Correct questions will be green, while incorrect will be blue
  • Your score will be displayed down tthe bottom, with a personalised repsonse
  • Correct answers are worth 2 points.
